Description

This is the OFFICIAL and ORIGINAL 2048 game created by Gabrielle Cirulli. Join the numbers to reach the 2048 tile and beyond!

Simply swipe to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one. Reach the 2048 tile and keep playing to reach the 4092 and even higher value tiles!

Elegantly designed with undo, detailed statistics, silky-smooth play and key features including:

• Classic play or Time trial
• Swipe anywhere to move tiles
• Tap undo to reverse a swipe
• Beautiful high-resolution graphics
• Large, easy to read board
• Detailed statistics
• Personal best scores
• Sounds with on/off switch
• Available for iPhone, iPad and iPod touch
• Official version of the original puzzle game
• Free to play

Published by the team responsible for award winning apps including Letterpress and Solebon Solitaire. This game is available for iPhone, iPad and iPod touch devices.

For support and answers to frequently asked questions, head over to http://www.2048original.com/support.html.

Sparrowhawk996 , 10/20/2017

Poor swipe detection

A slightly diagonal swipe can get interpreted as a different direction than you meant. The game needs to wait for a little bit longer swipe before moving the tiles. I've played several 2048 games, and this is the only one that misinterpreted swipes. I played this game once, up to 256, and it had already misinterpreted 2 swipes. Forget it, that's way too twitchy swipe detection for this kind of game.

UPDATE: the developer says I was touching the screen with multiple fingers, and to turn off the “extended swipe” feature. But I wasn’t touching with multiple fingers. I reinstalled the game and turned off extended swipe. The game still interprets even super-slight movement of my ONE fingertip as a full swipe. It’s still way too sensitive and interprets touch input before there’s enough input to accurately identify the overall direction of the swipe. I’m using an iPhone 6.

Developer Response ,

Try the new 3.0 version which includes updated swipe detection.

Why pay? , 08/18/2020

Ads ruin the experience

The game was great. I liked the colors on this better than others. Previously, the ads were bearable. They claim that the update didn’t add more ads — maybe they think that’s true, but the ads that existed before generally to upgrade the game. Occasionally there would be ads for other games. Now, there’s ads for all kinds of annoying stuff — one even tried to get me to turn on my location for 2048.

The location of the close ad button has changed. It used to be in the upper right corner consistently. Now, the close area unpredictability changes. More often than not, the click target is small. Both of these issues leads to accessibility clicking the ad.

The trickery is unacceptable. I’ve deleted this game from my phone
